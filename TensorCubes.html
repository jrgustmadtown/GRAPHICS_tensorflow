TensorFlow.js Neural Network Cube Learning

How it works:
• Red cube moves in a defined circle
• Purple cube learns to follow the red cube
• Blue cube learns to follow the purple cube
• Neural networks use 6 inputs (position + velocity)
• Output: 6 discrete actions (Up, Down, Left, Right, Forward, Back)
• Training uses a simple behavior function as ground truth

Phases:
Phase 1: 10s of direct following
Phase 2: Neural network learning begins
